About Us – Our Story

Founded with passion and dedication, Oak & Iron Carpentry has grown from a humble workshop into a trusted name in custom woodworking. For decades, we have combined traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to create pieces that stand the test of time.

As our company evolved, so did our responsibility toward the environment. Today, we proudly lead a Sustainable Carpentry Program designed to minimize our ecological footprint. This initiative is built on two pillars:

  • Tree Replanting Commitment: For every tree used in production, we actively support reforestation projects by funding and participating in the planting of new trees. This ensures that the resources we rely on are continuously renewed for future generations.

  • Raw Material Reuse: Whenever possible, we reclaim and repurpose wood from previous projects, old furniture, and responsibly sourced materials. By giving new life to existing resources, we reduce waste and honor the beauty of every piece of wood we work with.

  • Through this program, Oak & Iron Carpentry not only continues its tradition of quality craftsmanship but also embraces a future where innovation and sustainability walk hand in hand. Our mission is to create timeless designs that respect both our clients’ needs and the natural world that inspires us.

Our story started with the vision of James Whitmore, a young craftsman who opened his first small workshop in 1985. Working entirely on his own, James dedicated countless hours to perfecting his skills, building furniture by hand, and serving his very first clients with care and attention. His reputation for quality soon spread throughout the community.

After several years of dedication and steady growth, the workshop took its first major leap forward. By the mid-1990s, the business welcomed its first employees, expanding production capabilities while maintaining the same high standards of craftsmanship. This transition marked a new chapter, turning a one-man workshop into a small but thriving company.

Today, Oak & Iron Carpentry stands as a symbol of innovation and tradition. With advanced machinery, expanded facilities, and a team of skilled professionals, the company continues to grow while staying true to its founding values. The latest phase of expansion focuses on state-of-the-art infrastructure and sustainable practices, ensuring that every project reflects both heritage and progress.
